About

In central New Jersey, the Imperial Donuts Shop is a socially-conscious dessert caterer and vendor, preparing miniature made-to-order donuts with elegant and unexpected toppings. The Imperial Donuts Shop aspires to revitalize local communities by building lasting relationships with towns and small business, hiring ethically, sourcing locally, and honoring the greatness inherent in every community.
